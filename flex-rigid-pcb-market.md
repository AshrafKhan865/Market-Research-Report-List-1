<p><p>The Flex Rigid PCB market is categorized into three main types: Single Layer, Double Layer, and Multi-Layer. Single Layer PCBs feature one conductive layer, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity for basic applications. Double Layer PCBs incorporate two conductive layers, enhancing design complexity and performance, suitable for more advanced electronics. Multi-Layer PCBs consist of three or more layers, providing high-density interconnections and superior functionality, making them ideal for intricate devices in sectors like telecommunications, automotive, and medical technology.</p></p>
