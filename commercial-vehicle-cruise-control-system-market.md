<p><p>The commercial vehicle cruise control system market is categorized into two main types: semi-automatic control and full automatic control. Semi-automatic control allows drivers to maintain a set speed while retaining some manual control over acceleration and braking, enhancing safety and driver engagement. In contrast, full automatic control systems autonomously manage speed, acceleration, and braking, optimizing fuel efficiency and reducing driver workload. Both types aim to improve driving comfort and reduce fatigue during long-distance travel, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
