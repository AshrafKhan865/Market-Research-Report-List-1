<p><strong>The Epoxy Curatives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acidic</li><li>Alkaline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy curatives market is categorized into acidic and alkaline types based on their chemical properties and reactions with epoxy resins. Acidic curatives, such as anhydrides and phenolic hardeners, facilitate cross-linking through acid-catalyzed reactions, resulting in durable coatings and adhesives. Alkaline curatives, including amines and amides, promote curing through base-catalyzed mechanisms, enhancing flexibility and toughness. Each type serves specific applications across industries, such as construction, automotive, and electronics, influencing performance characteristics of the final products.</p></p>
