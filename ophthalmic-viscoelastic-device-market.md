<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cohesive OVDs</li><li>Dispersive OVDs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmic viscoelastic device (OVD) market comprises two primary types: cohesive OVDs and dispersive OVDs. Cohesive OVDs feature a high molecular weight, providing excellent visibility during surgery and effective maintenance of anterior chamber depth. They are ideal for techniques requiring controlled dissection. In contrast, dispersive OVDs possess lower viscosity and better adaptability to ocular tissues, offering enhanced protection for corneal endothelium and reducing trauma during surgical procedures. Both types are crucial for optimizing outcomes in cataract and other ocular surgeries.</p></p>
