<p><ul><li>Single-Idler</li><li>Two-Idler</li><li>Multi-Idler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conveyor scales are classified into three main types based on their design and idler configurations: single-idler, two-idler, and multi-idler scales. Single-idler scales use one idler to support the conveyor belt and measure weight, suitable for simpler applications. Two-idler scales provide improved accuracy and stability, utilizing two idlers for better load distribution. Multi-idler scales offer the highest precision, employing multiple idlers to accommodate various material loads and enhance measurement accuracy across diverse industrial settings.</p></p>
