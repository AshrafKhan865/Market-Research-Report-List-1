<p><strong>The Oral Guided Bone Regeneration (GTR) Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Membrane</li><li>Non-absorbable Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oral Guided Bone Regeneration (GTR) membrane market comprises two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes, made from materials that dissolve over time, eliminate the need for a second surgery and promote natural tissue healing. In contrast, non-absorbable membranes are durable and provide long-term support, requiring surgical removal after the healing process. Both types are essential for enhancing bone regeneration in oral surgeries, improving outcomes, and supporting dental implant procedures.</p></p>
