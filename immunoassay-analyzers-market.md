<p><strong>The Immunoassay Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunofluorescence</li><li>Chemiluminescence</li><li>ELISA</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Immunoassay analyzers encompass various technologies for detecting and quantifying specific biomolecules. Immunofluorescence utilizes fluorescently labeled antibodies to visualize target substances in samples. Chemiluminescence employs chemical reactions to produce light, enhancing sensitivity. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) involves antigen-antibody interactions with enzyme-linked detection, commonly used for diagnostics. Radioimmunoassay uses radioactively labeled antibodies to identify molecular targets, although less common today due to safety concerns. Each type offers distinct advantages for clinical and research applications, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
