<p><p>Microwave Power Transmission Systems primarily consist of two key market types: induction and magnetic resonance. Induction uses electromagnetic fields to transfer energy wirelessly between coils, enabling efficient power transfer over short distances, ideal for applications like charging electric vehicles. In contrast, magnetic resonance utilizes resonant energy transfer between two tuned circuits, allowing for efficient and safe power transmission over greater distances and through obstacles. Both technologies hold significant potential for various industries, enhancing energy efficiency and enabling innovative applications.</p></p>
