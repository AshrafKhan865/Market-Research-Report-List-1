<p><p>The Specialty Rotary Bearings market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Ball Bearings** use spherical balls to reduce friction, suitable for high-speed applications. </p><p>2. **Roller Bearings** utilize cylindrical rollers, providing higher load capacity and stability. </p><p>3. **Plain Bearings**, or sleeve bearings, have a simple design without moving parts and are used in low-speed, high-load applications. </p><p>4. **Others** encompass specialized bearings designed for unique applications, such as magnetic or fluid bearings, catering to niche market needs.</p></p>
