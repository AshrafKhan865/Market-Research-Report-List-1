<p><p>The Serum-Free Freezing Culture Media market includes two primary types: DMSO-based and DMSO-free solutions. DMSO (Dimethyl sulfoxide) is commonly used as a cryoprotectant to prevent ice crystal formation during the freezing process, enhancing cell viability post-thaw. DMSO-free media, on the other hand, caters to those seeking alternatives due to potential cytotoxicity or ethical concerns regarding animal-derived products. Both types are essential for optimizing the preservation of various cell types in research and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
