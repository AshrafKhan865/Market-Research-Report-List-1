<p><strong>The Vehicle Lead Acid Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Battery</li><li>Valve Regulated Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle lead-acid battery market comprises two main types: open batteries and valve-regulated batteries (VRLA). Open batteries, often used in traditional automotive applications, rely on liquid electrolyte and require regular maintenance, including topping up with water. In contrast, VRLA batteries are sealed, using gel or absorbed glass mat technologies to prevent spillage and eliminate maintenance needs. VRLA batteries are increasingly popular due to their reliability and safety features, making them suitable for modern vehicles, including electric and hybrid models.</p></p>
