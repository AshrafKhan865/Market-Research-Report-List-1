<p><p>The screws for bone fixation market includes three main types: stainless steel, titanium, and bioabsorbable screws. Stainless steel screws offer strength and durability, making them a popular choice for many orthopedic procedures. Titanium screws are favored for their biocompatibility, lightweight nature, and resistance to corrosion, reducing the risk of allergic reactions. Bioabsorbable screws, made from polymers, gradually dissolve in the body, eliminating the need for removal surgeries and promoting natural healing, thus appealing for pediatric applications and specific orthopedic cases.</p></p>
