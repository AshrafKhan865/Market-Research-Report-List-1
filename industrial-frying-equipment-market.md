<p><p>The industrial frying equipment market is segmented into three main types: continuous, intermittent, and other systems. Continuous frying equipment allows for a steady flow of products through the fryer, ensuring uniform cooking and higher efficiency for mass production. Intermittent frying equipment, on the other hand, operates in batches, making it suitable for smaller volumes or specialized products. Other types may include combinations of both methods or niche technologies catering to specific frying needs, offering flexibility and adaptability in varying production environments.</p></p>
