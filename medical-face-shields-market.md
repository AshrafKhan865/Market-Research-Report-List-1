<p><ul><li>3/4 Length</li><li>Full Length</li><li>Half Length</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical face shields market can be categorized into three main types based on coverage: half-length, full-length, and 3/4 length. Half-length shields offer basic protection, covering the face from the forehead to the chin, ideal for tasks with minimal splash risk. Full-length shields provide comprehensive coverage, extending beyond the neck, suitable for high-risk environments. Meanwhile, 3/4 length shields balance coverage and comfort, protecting the upper face while allowing for better visibility and ventilation, making them versatile for various healthcare settings.</p></p>
