<p><p>The SOC Hardmask market consists of two primary types: Hot-Temperature Spin on Carbon (HT-SOC) Hardmask and Normal Spin on Carbon (N-SOC) Hardmask. HT-SOC is designed for advanced lithography processes, capable of withstanding elevated temperatures, making it suitable for high-performance applications in semiconductor manufacturing. N-SOC, on the other hand, operates at standard temperatures, providing cost-effective solutions for less demanding applications. Both types play critical roles in creating precise features and patterns on various substrates in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
