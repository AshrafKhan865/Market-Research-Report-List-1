<p><strong>The MEMS Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Pressure Sensors</li><li>Piezoresistive Pressure Sensors</li><li>Piezoelectric Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS pressure sensor market comprises three main types: capacitive, piezoresistive, and piezoelectric sensors. Capacitive pressure sensors measure variations in capacitance based on pressure-induced changes in diaphragm distance. Piezoresistive sensors utilize the change in electrical resistance of materials when subjected to stress, offering high accuracy for pressure measurements. Piezoelectric sensors generate an electrical charge in response to pressure changes, suitable for dynamic pressure monitoring. Each type serves distinct applications in automotive, medical, and industrial sectors, driving market growth.</p></p>
