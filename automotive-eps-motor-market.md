<p><strong>The Automotive EPS Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive EPS (Electric Power Steering) motor market includes four main types: CEPS (Column Electric Power Steering) motors, which are compact and integrated within the steering column; REPS (Rack Electric Power Steering) motors, located directly on the steering rack for precise control; PEPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering) motors, positioned on the pinion gear for improved energy efficiency; and EHPS (Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering) motors, which combine electric and hydraulic systems to enhance steering performance. Each offers unique benefits in terms of design and functionality.</p></p>
