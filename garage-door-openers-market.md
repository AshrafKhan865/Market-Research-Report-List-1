<p><p>The garage door openers market features three primary types: belt drive, chain drive, and screw drive openers, along with other specialized options. Belt drive openers utilize a belt for quiet operation, ideal for homes attached to garages. Chain drive openers, known for their durability and affordability, operate using a metal chain, making them suitable for heavy doors. Screw drive openers use a threaded steel rod for movement, offering low maintenance. Other options may include smart or direct current models catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
