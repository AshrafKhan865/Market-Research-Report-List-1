<p><p>The specialty contact lenses market includes three main types: rigid contact lenses, hybrid contact lenses, and soft contact lenses. Rigid contact lenses are durable and provide clear vision, often used for conditions like astigmatism. Hybrid contact lenses combine a rigid center with a soft outer ring for comfort and clarity. Soft contact lenses are made from flexible materials, offering ease of use and varying designs for different vision needs. Each type caters to specific requirements, enhancing visual correction and comfort.</p></p>
