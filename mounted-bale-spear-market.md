<p><p>The mounted bale spear market is categorized into three main types: single-fork, double-fork, and multi-fork. Single-fork models are designed for handling one bale at a time, offering simplicity and ease of use. Double-fork variants allow for improved stability and efficiency by lifting two bales simultaneously. Multi-fork options provide the versatility to handle several bales at once, catering to larger operations requiring high productivity. Each type serves distinct operational needs in agriculture and material handling.</p></p>
