<p><p>The aquaculture fish cage market consists of two main types: plastic cages and metal cages. They are often used for smaller operations and in freshwater environments. In contrast, metal cages, typically made of galvanized steel or aluminum, provide durability and strength, making them ideal for larger-scale operations and harsher marine environments. Each type serves specific aquaculture needs based on operational scale and environmental factors.</p></p>
