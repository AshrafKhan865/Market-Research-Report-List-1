<p><ul><li>Single Acting Gylinders</li><li>Double Acting Cylinders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telescopic hydraulic cylinders market includes single-acting and double-acting cylinders. Single-acting cylinders use hydraulic pressure to extend, while a spring or gravity retracts them, making them suitable for lifting applications where space is limited. Double-acting cylinders operate on both extension and retraction via hydraulic pressure, providing greater force and control, ideal for more complex tasks that require movement in both directions. Both types are commonly used in construction, mining, and material handling, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
