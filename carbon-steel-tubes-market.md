<p><strong>The Carbon Steel Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Welded Tubes</li><li>Seamless Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon steel tubes market is primarily categorized into welded and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are produced by rolling steel strips and welding the seams, making them suitable for high-volume applications where cost efficiency is critical. Seamless tubes, on the other hand, are manufactured from solid round billets, providing enhanced strength and resistance to pressure, making them ideal for high-stress environments like oil and gas industries. Each type serves distinct applications based on structural and material requirements.</p></p>
