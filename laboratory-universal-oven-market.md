<p><strong>The Laboratory Universal Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Convection</li><li>Forced Convection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laboratory Universal Oven market is categorized into two main types: natural convection and forced convection ovens. Natural convection ovens rely on the natural circulation of heated air, which gently distributes warmth throughout the chamber, making them suitable for delicate drying processes. In contrast, forced convection ovens utilize fans to actively circulate hot air, ensuring uniform heat distribution and faster drying times. Each type serves distinct applications in laboratories, catering to specific research and industrial needs.</p></p>
