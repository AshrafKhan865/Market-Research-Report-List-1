<p><ul><li>Far</li><li>Medium</li><li>Near</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floor-standing infrared heaters are categorized by the wavelength of infrared radiation they emit: far, medium, and near. Far infrared heaters emit long wavelengths that penetrate deep into the skin, providing effective warmth for larger spaces. Medium infrared heaters offer a balance, ideal for both heating spaces and providing comfort. Near infrared heaters produce short wavelengths, delivering immediate warmth and light, making them suitable for smaller areas or targeted heating, such as in bathrooms or workshops. Each type serves specific heating needs effectively.</p></p>
