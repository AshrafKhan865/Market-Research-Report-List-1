<p><ul><li>Single-beam</li><li>Multi-beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial side scan sonar market is primarily segmented into single-beam and multi-beam systems. Single-beam sonar provides depth measurements from a single point, making it suitable for simple surveys and detailed analysis of the seafloor. In contrast, multi-beam sonar uses multiple beams to capture wider swathes of data simultaneously, offering detailed and comprehensive underwater mapping. Each type serves distinct applications such as marine exploration, environmental monitoring, and underwater construction, driving advancements in underwater imaging technology.</p></p>
