<p><p>The Double Layer Ore Sorter market encompasses two primary types: dry type and wet type. Dry type sorters utilize air jets to separate materials based on density and size without the need for water, making them suitable for arid regions and minimizing water usage. Conversely, wet type sorters employ water to enhance separation efficiency, particularly for finer materials, allowing for better sorting precision. Both types cater to different operational needs and mineral characteristics, contributing to the overall efficiency of ore processing.</p></p>
