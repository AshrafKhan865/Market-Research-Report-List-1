<p><p>The 5G Telematics Control Unit (TCU) market is segmented into two primary types: OEM and aftermarket. OEM TCUs are integrated directly into vehicles by manufacturers during production, providing advanced connectivity and features from the start. In contrast, aftermarket TCUs are installed post-manufacturing, allowing vehicle owners to upgrade connectivity capabilities or enhance vehicle performance. The OEM segment typically ensures seamless integration with existing systems, while the aftermarket offers flexibility and customization for consumers seeking enhanced telematics solutions.</p></p>
