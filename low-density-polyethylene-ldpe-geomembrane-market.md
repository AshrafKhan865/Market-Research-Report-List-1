<p><p>The Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) geomembrane market is segmented into three main types: single layer, double layer, and three layer geomembranes. Single layer geomembranes consist of a single sheet of LDPE, offering basic waterproofing and flexibility. Double layer geomembranes provide enhanced strength and durability through two sheets, ideal for applications requiring more robust barriers. Three layer geomembranes incorporate an additional middle layer for improved mechanical properties and resistance, making them suitable for demanding environments in waste containment and water management.</p></p>
