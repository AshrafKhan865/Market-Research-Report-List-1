<p><p>The stethoscope market is primarily categorized into manual, mechanical, and digital stethoscopes. Manual and mechanical stethoscopes utilize traditional acoustic principles, allowing healthcare professionals to listen directly to body sounds via tubing and a chest piece. Digital stethoscopes, on the other hand, leverage advanced technology to amplify sound and may include features like recording and digital analysis. Each type serves specific clinical needs, with manual and mechanical stethoscopes emphasizing simplicity and reliability, while digital variants offer enhanced diagnostics and convenience.</p></p>
