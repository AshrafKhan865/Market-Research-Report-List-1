<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Leak Detector</li><li>Helium Leak Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vacuum Leak Test System market comprises primarily two types: Ultrasonic Leak Detectors and Helium Leak Detectors. Ultrasonic Leak Detectors identify leaks by capturing high-frequency sound waves generated by escaping gases. These are ideal for applications where quick, non-invasive detection is necessary. Helium Leak Detectors utilize helium as a tracer gas, providing extremely sensitive leak detection in vacuum environments. This method is particularly effective for industries like aerospace and pharmaceuticals, where precise integrity testing is critical.</p></p>
