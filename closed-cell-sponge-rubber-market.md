<p><p>The closed-cell sponge rubber market encompasses various types, each with unique properties and applications. Nitrile rubber provides superior oil resistance, ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Neoprene combines flexibility and resistance to various chemicals, widely used in insulation and sealing. Silicone rubber boasts high-temperature stability and flexibility, making it suitable for diverse environments. Other types may include natural rubber and specialty compounds, catering to niche markets.</p></p>
