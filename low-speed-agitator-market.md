<p><strong>The Low Speed Agitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anchor Impeller</li><li>Helical Impeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-speed agitator market is primarily categorized into two types: anchor impellers and helical impellers. Anchor impellers have a broad, flat design, ideal for thick fluids, ensuring effective mixing by scraping the vessel walls. They are commonly used in applications requiring high viscosity processing. Helical impellers feature a spiral design that promotes smooth fluid flow, making them suitable for low-viscosity liquids. Both types play crucial roles in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemical manufacturing, optimizing mixing performance.</p></p>
