<p><p>The Antimicrobial Growth Promoters market is categorized into several types, including Penicillins, Lincosamides, Macrolides, and Others. The "Others" category includes various antimicrobial agents not classified under these groups, catering to specific veterinary needs, enhancing growth and health in livestock.</p></p>
