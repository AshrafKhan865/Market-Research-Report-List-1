<p><strong>The Highway Crash Cushion Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-redirective Crash Cushions</li><li>Redirective Crash Cushions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The highway crash cushion market is segmented into two main types: non-redirective and redirective crash cushions. Non-redirective crash cushions absorb impact energy without redirecting errant vehicles, making them ideal for locations where lateral space is limited. In contrast, redirective crash cushions are designed to absorb impact while guiding vehicles back onto the roadway, suitable for areas with more space and higher speeds. Both types aim to enhance road safety by minimizing injury and damage during vehicular collisions.</p></p>
