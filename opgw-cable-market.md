<p><ul><li>Layer Stranding Structure</li><li>Central Tube Design</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OPGW (Optical Fiber Composite Overhead Ground Wire) cable market comprises various designs, notably the Layer Stranding Structure and Central Tube Design. The Layer Stranding Structure features multiple layers of optical fibers stranded around a central core, ensuring flexibility and strength. In contrast, the Central Tube Design houses fibers within a central tube, providing enhanced protection and ease of installation. Both designs are tailored to meet diverse operational requirements, emphasizing durability and efficiency in power transmission and telecommunications.</p></p>
