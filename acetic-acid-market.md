<p><p>The acetic acid market is primarily divided into two types: standard acetic acid and glacial acetic acid. Standard acetic acid, typically containing 36% to 38% concentration, is widely used in food preservation and as a chemical reagent. In contrast, glacial acetic acid, with a purity of more than 98%, is utilized in chemical synthesis, pharmaceuticals, and as a solvent. The distinction between these types is crucial for meeting specific industrial applications and regulatory standards.</p></p>
