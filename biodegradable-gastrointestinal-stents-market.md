<p><p>The biodegradable gastrointestinal stents market consists of various types, each designed for specific applications. Biliary stents facilitate bile flow in patients with obstructions. Duodenal stents help alleviate gastric outlet obstructions, while colonic stents are used for treating colorectal obstructions. Pancreatic stents are meant for drainage in cases of pancreatitis or strictures, and esophageal stents are employed to relieve swallowing difficulties due to tumors or strictures. These stents are designed to degrade over time, minimizing the need for additional procedures.</p></p>
