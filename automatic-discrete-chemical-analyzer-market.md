<p><strong>The Automatic Discrete Chemical Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flow Cell Based System</li><li>Direct Read System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Discrete Chemical Analyzer market includes two primary types: Flow Cell Based Systems and Direct Read Systems. Flow Cell Based Systems utilize continuous flow technology to analyze samples, allowing for high-throughput and efficient processing of multiple tests simultaneously. In contrast, Direct Read Systems perform analyses directly on discrete sample volumes, offering rapid results without the need for complex fluid handling. Both systems are essential in laboratories for streamlining chemical analysis, improving accuracy, and enhancing overall productivity.</p></p>
