<p><p>Linear Alkylbenzene Sulfonate (LAS) is a widely used surfactant in detergents and cleaning products, known for its excellent wetting properties and biodegradability. The market is divided into two main types: Hard LAS and Soft LAS. Hard LAS is characterized by a higher carbon chain length, providing stronger cleaning power but less biodegradability. In contrast, Soft LAS has shorter carbon chains, offering improved environmental compatibility and is more easily biodegradable, making it preferable for eco-friendly formulations.</p></p>
