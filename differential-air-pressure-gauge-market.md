<p><ul><li>Piston</li><li>Diaphragm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Differential Air Pressure Gauge market primarily consists of two main types: piston and diaphragm gauges. Piston gauges utilize a piston mechanism to measure pressure differences, providing accurate readings in various industrial applications. Diaphragm gauges, on the other hand, employ a flexible diaphragm that responds to pressure changes, offering reliability and sensitivity. Both types are essential in monitoring air pressure in HVAC systems, cleanrooms, and other environments where maintaining specific pressure differentials is critical for operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
