<p><p>The ceramic packing membrane market encompasses various types of membranes, including plate type, tubular type, and multichannel ceramic membranes. Plate type ceramic membranes are flat and facilitate high surface area for efficient filtration. Tubular type membranes feature a tubular structure, allowing for higher flow rates and reduced fouling. Multichannel ceramic membranes combine multiple channels within a single framework, optimizing space and performance for complex filtration processes. Each type serves distinct applications in industries such as water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
