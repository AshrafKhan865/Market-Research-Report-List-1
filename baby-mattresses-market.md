<p><p>The baby mattress market encompasses primarily two types: foam and innerspring. Foam mattresses are known for their lightweight, support, and contouring characteristics, providing comfort for infants while reducing the risk of suffocation. Innerspring mattresses feature coils for added firmness and airflow, promoting a cooler sleeping environment. Each type offers unique benefits; foam tends to be more portable and easily compressed, while innerspring options often provide enhanced durability and bounce. Parents choose based on safety, comfort, and practical considerations.</p></p>
