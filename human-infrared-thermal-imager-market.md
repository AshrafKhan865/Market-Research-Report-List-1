<p><strong>The Human Infrared Thermal Imager Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photon Detection</li><li>Thermal Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Human Infrared Thermal Imager market encompasses two primary types: photon detection and thermal detection. Photon detection relies on the absorption of infrared radiation by materials to generate images, often resulting in high-resolution visuals suitable for various applications. In contrast, thermal detection measures the heat emitted by objects, creating images based on temperature differences, which is effective in low-light or no-light conditions. Each detection type serves distinct needs in industries like security, medical diagnostics, and firefighting.</p></p>
