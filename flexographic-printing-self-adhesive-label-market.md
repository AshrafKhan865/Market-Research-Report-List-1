<p><p>The flexographic printing self-adhesive label market includes two primary types: release liner labels and liner-less labels. Release liner labels are backed by a silicone-coated material, allowing easy peeling and application, often used for a variety of labeling needs. Liner-less labels, on the other hand, eliminate the backing paper, making them more environmentally friendly and cost-effective, as they require less material and are easier to dispense. Both types cater to different industry requirements, enhancing efficiency and sustainability in labeling solutions.</p></p>
