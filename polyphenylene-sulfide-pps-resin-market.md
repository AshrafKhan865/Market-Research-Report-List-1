<p><p>The Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) Resin market is primarily divided into two types: linear and cross-linked. Linear PPS is characterized by its flexible chain structure, offering excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability, making it suitable for applications in automotive and electronics. In contrast, cross-linked PPS features a networked structure, resulting in enhanced rigidity and heat resistance, ideal for high-performance applications in aerospace and industrial sectors. Both types cater to diverse industrial demands, driving their market growth.</p></p>
