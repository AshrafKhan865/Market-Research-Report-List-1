<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Head-Up Display (HUD) system market primarily consists of two types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUDs project vital information directly onto the vehicle's windshield, allowing drivers to maintain focus on the road while viewing data such as speed and navigation. In contrast, Combiner Projected HUDs use a smaller, transparent screen that retracts from the dashboard to display information, offering a less intrusive alternative. Both systems enhance driving safety and convenience by providing critical information within the driver’s line of sight.</p></p>
