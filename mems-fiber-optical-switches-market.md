<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber Optic Switch</li><li>Multimode Fiber Optic Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS fiber optic switch market is primarily divided into two types: single mode fiber optic switches and multimode fiber optic switches. Single mode fiber optic switches are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path to minimize signal loss and dispersion, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers. Multimode fiber optic switches, on the other hand, support multiple light paths, providing higher data rates over shorter distances, which is suitable for local area networks and enterprise applications.</p></p>
