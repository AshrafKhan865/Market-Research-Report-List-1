<p><p>The ISO Pet Microchip market comprises three primary types based on frequency: 125 kHz, 128 kHz, and 134 kHz microchips. The 125 kHz microchip, while historically used, is less common today due to compatibility issues with international standards. The 128 kHz and 134 kHz microchips are more widely accepted, with the 134 kHz variant being the ISO-standard chip for pet identification, ensuring reliable tracking and recovery of lost pets globally. Each type serves the essential purpose of pet identification and safety.</p></p>
