<p><ul><li>Threshold Monitoring</li><li>Comprehensive Component Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The On-board Diagnostic (OBD) system market encompasses two primary types: Threshold Monitoring and Comprehensive Component Monitoring. Threshold Monitoring focuses on detecting when vehicle parameters exceed predefined limits, promptly alerting drivers to potential issues. In contrast, Comprehensive Component Monitoring provides a detailed analysis of various vehicle components, enabling continuous evaluation of performance and emissions over time. Both types enhance vehicle reliability and efficiency, contributing to improved maintenance strategies and regulatory compliance within the automotive industry.</p></p>
