<p><ul><li>Polymer Type</li><li>Non-polymer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Filtration Control Additive market can be divided into two main types: Polymer Type and Non-Polymer Type. Polymer Type additives, such as celluloses or synthetic polymers, enhance fluid properties and improve filtration efficiency by forming a gel-like barrier. Non-Polymer Type additives, including natural clays and chemical agents, usually aid in controlling fluid loss and stabilizing suspensions without the use of synthetic materials. Both types are essential for optimizing drilling operations and ensuring effective fluid management in various industries.</p></p>
