<p><strong>The Mobile Hemodialysis Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pump Hemodialysis Machine</li><li>Double Pump Hemodialysis Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile hemodialysis machine market comprises two main types: single pump and double pump machines. Single pump hemodialysis machines utilize one pump for both blood circulation and dialysate flow, making them compact and user-friendly, which is ideal for at-home treatment. In contrast, double pump machines feature separate pumps for blood and dialysate, providing enhanced efficiency and precision in dialysis treatment. Both types cater to the growing demand for portable, convenient solutions for patients requiring renal care outside traditional clinical settings.</p></p>
