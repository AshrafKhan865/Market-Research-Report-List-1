<p><strong>The HDMI Optical Transceivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber</li><li>Double Mode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDMI Optical Transceivers market is categorized into two main types: Single Mode Fiber (SMF) and Double Mode Fiber (DMF). Single Mode Fiber transceivers are designed for long-range communication, utilizing a single light path to minimize signal loss and maximize transmission distance, ideally suited for large installations. In contrast, Double Mode Fiber transceivers employ two different light paths, offering increased bandwidth and versatility for shorter distances, making them suitable for various applications where higher capacity is needed over moderate ranges.</p></p>
