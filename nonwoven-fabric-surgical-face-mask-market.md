<p><p>The nonwoven fabric surgical face mask market primarily comprises two types: single-layer and double-layer masks. Single-layer masks consist of one sheet of nonwoven fabric, offering basic protection and breathability, suitable for low-risk environments. In contrast, double-layer masks are made from two layers of nonwoven material, providing enhanced filtration and barrier protection against airborne particles and pathogens, making them ideal for higher-risk situations. The choice between these types depends on the specific application and required level of protection.</p></p>
