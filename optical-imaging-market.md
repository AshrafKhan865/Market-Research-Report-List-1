<p><strong>The Optical Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)</li><li>Photoacoustic Imaging</li><li>Diffused Optical Tomography</li><li>Hyperspectral Imaging</li><li>Near-Infrared Spectroscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical imaging market encompasses various advanced imaging techniques. Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) offers high-resolution cross-sectional imaging, primarily used in ophthalmology. Photoacoustic imaging combines light and sound for functional tissue visualization. Diffused Optical Tomography provides three-dimensional imaging by analyzing the scattering of near-infrared light. Hyperspectral imaging captures a broad spectrum of light per pixel, allowing detailed material characterization. Near-Infrared Spectroscopy assesses the composition of materials based on light absorption, commonly utilized in medical diagnostics and food quality analysis.</p></p>
