<p><ul><li>Traditional TV Remote Control</li><li>Universal Remote Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TV remote controller market consists of traditional and universal remote controls. Traditional TV remote controls are designed specifically for a single television brand or model, offering basic functionalities like power, volume, and channel control. In contrast, universal remote controllers are versatile devices that can operate multiple devices, including TVs, sound systems, and streaming devices, consolidating functionality into one unit. This flexibility appeals to consumers seeking convenience and reduced clutter, leading to a growing preference for universal remotes in modern households.</p></p>
