<p><strong>The Endoscope Disinfectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Chamber</li><li>Multi Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscope disinfectors market is categorized into single chamber and multi-chamber systems. Single chamber disinfectors utilize one compartment for the entire cleaning and disinfection process, offering simplicity and efficiency for smaller facilities. In contrast, multi-chamber systems feature distinct compartments for pre-cleaning, cleaning, and disinfection, enabling simultaneous processing of multiple endoscopes. This enhances workflow efficiency and reduces turnaround times, making them ideal for larger healthcare facilities with high-volume endoscope usage. Both types ensure effective sterilization and infection control.</p></p>
