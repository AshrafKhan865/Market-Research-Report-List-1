<p><p>Filtering Facepiece Respirators (FFRs) are categorized into two main types: those without a valve and those with a valve. FFRs without a valve offer thorough filtration but can retain heat and moisture, potentially causing discomfort during prolonged use. In contrast, FFRs with a valve facilitate easier exhalation, enhancing comfort and reducing heat buildup, particularly in high-intensity situations. Both types effectively filter airborne particles, but their choice depends on the specific needs of users regarding comfort and protection levels.</p></p>
