<p><p>The RNA Sequencing Technologies market is primarily divided into two main types: Non-Coding RNA Sequencing and Direct RNA Sequencing. Non-Coding RNA Sequencing focuses on analyzing RNA molecules that do not code for proteins but play critical regulatory roles in gene expression and cellular processes. Direct RNA Sequencing captures the native sequence of RNA directly, preserving base modifications and enabling real-time analysis. Both technologies provide valuable insights into gene regulation, cellular function, and disease mechanisms, driving advancements in genomics research.</p></p>
