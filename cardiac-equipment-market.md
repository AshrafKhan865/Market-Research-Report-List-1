<p><p>The cardiac equipment market includes a range of devices designed to support heart function. Balloon pumps assist in managing blood flow during procedures, while cardiopulmonary bypass pumps facilitate heart surgery by maintaining circulation. Cardiac ultrasound devices provide imaging for diagnosis. CRT Pacemakers (CRT-P) and CRT Defibrillators (CRT-D) are specialized devices for heart rhythm management. Traditional pacemakers regulate heartbeats, while defibrillators deliver shocks to restore normal rhythm. Ventricular assist devices support patients with severe heart failure, improving their quality of life.</p></p>
