<p><p>The Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) scanners market includes various types such as Spectral Domain (SD) OCT and Swept Source (SS) OCT. SD OCT utilizes a spectrometer to capture images, providing high-resolution cross-sectional views, ideal for applications like ophthalmology. SS OCT employs a tunable laser source, allowing for deeper tissue imaging and faster scanning speeds, making it suitable for both research and clinical settings. Other market types may include time-domain OCT and portable OCT systems, catering to diverse imaging needs.</p></p>
