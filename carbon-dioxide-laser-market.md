<p><strong>The Carbon Dioxide Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Output</li><li>Pulse Output</li><li>Q Switch Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon dioxide laser market is categorized into three main types: continuous output, pulse output, and Q-switch output. Continuous output lasers emit a steady beam, suitable for applications requiring consistent energy delivery, such as cutting and engraving. Pulse output lasers deliver energy in short bursts, ideal for precision tasks like surgeries or material processing. Q-switch output lasers can produce high-energy pulses, making them effective for applications like tattoo removal and industrial marking, offering deeper tissue penetration and enhanced effectiveness.</p></p>
