<p><strong>The High Viscosity Tissue Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-OCA</li><li>n-2BCA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high viscosity tissue adhesives market primarily consists of two key types: 2-OCA (2-octyl cyanoacrylate) and n-2BCA (n-butyl cyanoacrylate). 2-OCA is known for its flexible bonding properties, making it ideal for skin closure in surgical procedures. In contrast, n-2BCA offers rapid polymerization and strong adhesion, suitable for a variety of tissues. Both types are significant in wound management, surgical applications, and trauma care, driven by their effectiveness, ease of use, and reduced infection risk.</p></p>
