<p><p>The Coronary Artery Disease (CAD) therapeutics market comprises various drug classes targeting heart health. Beta-blockers reduce heart rate and blood pressure, improving cardiac output. Calcium channel blockers relax blood vessels, enhancing blood flow and reducing angina. Nitrates dilate blood vessels, alleviating chest pain. ACE inhibitors help relax blood vessels by inhibiting a hormone that narrows them, lowering blood pressure. Other treatments may include antiplatelet agents and statins, aiming to reduce cholesterol and prevent clot formation, enhancing overall cardiac function and health.</p></p>
