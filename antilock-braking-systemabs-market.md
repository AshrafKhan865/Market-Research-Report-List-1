<p><p>The Antilock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lock-up during braking, enhancing vehicle control and safety. "Three Leading" features three sensors, usually on the front wheels, providing good control. "Two Leading" has two sensors, often used in cost-effective setups, while "One Leading" involves a single sensor, offering basic ABS functionality for simpler vehicles. Each type balances cost and performance.</p></p>
