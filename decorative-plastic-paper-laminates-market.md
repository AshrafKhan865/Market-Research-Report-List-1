<p><p>The decorative plastic paper laminates market comprises three main types: low pressure, high pressure, and edge banding. Low pressure laminates are cost-effective and typically used for furniture and wall panels, offering moderate durability and design options. High pressure laminates provide superior strength and resistance, making them suitable for heavy-use surfaces like countertops. Edge banding, often made from the same laminate material, is used to cover exposed edges of panels, enhancing aesthetics and protecting against damage, thus ensuring a cohesive and durable finish.</p></p>
