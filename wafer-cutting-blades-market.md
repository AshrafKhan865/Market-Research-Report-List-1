<p><strong>The Wafer Cutting Blades Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hub Dicing Blades</li><li>Hubless Dicing Blades</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer cutting blades market is primarily divided into two types: hub dicing blades and hubless dicing blades. Hub dicing blades feature a central hub that allows for better stability and alignment during cutting, making them suitable for larger wafers and high-precision applications. In contrast, hubless dicing blades are designed without a central hub, offering flexibility and a reduced weight, which can enhance cutting speed and efficiency. Both types are essential for the semiconductor and photovoltaic industries, catering to diverse cutting needs.</p></p>
