<p><p>UHF RF mixers are essential components in communication systems, and they are categorized into passive and active mixers. Passive mixers rely on non-linear devices, such as diodes, to mix signals without requiring external power, offering high dynamic range and reliability. Active mixers, on the other hand, incorporate amplifying elements like transistors, enabling better performance in terms of gain and bandwidth, but often with increased complexity and power consumption. Both types serve distinct applications in RF communication and signal processing.</p></p>
