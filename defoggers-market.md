<p><p>The defoggers market features various types of mist eliminators, including wire-mesh mist eliminators and baffle-type mist climators. Wire-mesh mist eliminators utilize a network of wires to capture and coalesce droplets from gas streams, effectively reducing fog and enhancing visibility. On the other hand, baffle-type mist eliminators employ a series of baffles or barriers to redirect airflow, promoting droplet collision and collection. Both types are crucial in industrial applications to ensure efficient operation, prevent corrosion, and maintain product quality.</p></p>
