<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Polypropylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Extrusion Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Polypropylene (GFR-PP) market is primarily divided into two types based on manufacturing processes: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves injecting heated GFR-PP into a mold, allowing for intricate shapes and high production rates, making it ideal for complex components in automotive and consumer goods. Extrusion molding, on the other hand, involves forcing GFR-PP through a die to create continuous shapes like sheets or profiles, commonly used in packaging, construction, and industrial applications.</p></p>
