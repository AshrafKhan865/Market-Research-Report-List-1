<p><p>The electrical components market is divided into two main types: active components and passive components. Active components, such as semiconductors, transistors, and integrated circuits, are capable of controlling electric current and amplifying signals. In contrast, passive components, including resistors, capacitors, and inductors, do not generate energy but rather store or dissipate it. These components are essential in various applications, from consumer electronics to industrial machinery, influencing overall performance and efficiency in electronic systems.</p></p>
