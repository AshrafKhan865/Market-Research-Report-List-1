<p><p>The orthodontic bracket adhesives market comprises two main types: light-curable and chemical-curable adhesives. Light-curable adhesives require exposure to a specific wavelength of light to initiate the curing process, providing quick setting times and ease of use. In contrast, chemical-curable adhesives rely on a chemical reaction to harden, offering extended working times and strong bonding properties, making them suitable for various orthodontic applications. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring durable and effective attachment of brackets to teeth.</p></p>
