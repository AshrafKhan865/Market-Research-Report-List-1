<p><p>The seismic isolator market for buildings features various types designed to enhance structural resilience during earthquakes. </p><p>- **Natural Rubber Bearings (NRB)** are flexible supports made of layered rubber that absorb seismic energy. </p><p>- **Lead Rubber Bearings (LRB)** incorporate lead plugs within elastomeric bearings, providing superior damping. </p><p>- **High-Damping Rubber Bearings (HDR)** utilize high-damping materials to effectively dissipate energy. </p><p>- **Others** encompass innovative solutions like isolation pads and sliding bearings. Each type plays a vital role in minimizing building damage and improving safety during seismic events.</p></p>
