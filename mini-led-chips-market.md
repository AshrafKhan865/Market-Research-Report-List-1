<p><strong>The Mini LED Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip LED</li><li>Vertical Chip LED</li><li>Flip Chip LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mini LED chips market consists of three main types: Lateral Chip LED, Vertical Chip LED, and Flip Chip LED. Lateral Chip LEDs are characterized by their side-emitting capabilities, offering enhanced light uniformity. Vertical Chip LEDs utilize a vertical structure for better thermal management and efficiency, ideal for high-brightness applications. Flip Chip LEDs feature a unique design that eliminates wire bonds, improving performance and reducing size, making them suitable for compact display technologies. Each type caters to different applications and performance requirements.</p></p>
