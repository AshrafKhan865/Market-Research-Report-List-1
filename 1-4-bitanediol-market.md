<p><p>The 1,4-butanediol (BDO) market features several production processes. The Reppe process combines acetylene and formaldehyde, yielding high purity BDO. The Davy process, using butadiene and hydrogen, is efficient but less commonly used. The Butadiene process relies on converting butadiene directly, while the Propylene oxide process involves propylene as a primary feedstock. Each method's market is influenced by raw material availability, production costs, and environmental considerations, shaping BDO's overall supply and market dynamics.</p></p>
