<p><strong>The Coffee Crystals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coffee crystals market primarily encompasses two production methods: spray drying and freeze drying. Spray drying involves rapidly drying liquid coffee through hot air, resulting in fine powdered coffee crystals, ideal for instant coffee due to its efficiency and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, freeze drying preserves flavors and aromas by freezing brewed coffee and removing moisture in a vacuum, yielding higher-quality crystals. Both methods cater to consumer demand for convenience while impacting flavor profiles and market pricing differently.</p></p>
