<p><strong>The Railway Wheel-Sets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outboard Bearings Arrangement</li><li>Inboard Bearing Arrangement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway wheel-sets market includes two primary types based on bearing arrangements: outboard bearing arrangements and inboard bearing arrangements. Outboard bearing arrangements feature bearings positioned outside the wheel-sets, which facilitate easier maintenance and access, often enhancing performance and reducing wear. In contrast, inboard bearing arrangements have bearings located within the wheel-set, offering a more compact design and improved weight distribution, but may complicate maintenance. Both arrangements cater to different operational needs and preferences in rail transport.</p></p>
