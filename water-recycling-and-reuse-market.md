Chemical treatment technology involves using chemical reactions to purify water, often employing methods like coagulation and disinfection. Biological treatment technology utilizes microorganisms to decompose organic matter in wastewater, enhancing water quality. These technologies are essential for sustainable water management, contributing to environmental conservation and resource efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
