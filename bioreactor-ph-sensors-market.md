<p><p>The bioreactor pH sensors market comprises several types, including glass type sensors, ISFET sensors, and others. Glass type sensors are traditional pH measurement tools known for their accuracy and reliability in various bioprocesses. ISFET sensors, or ion-sensitive field-effect transistors, offer enhanced durability and faster response times, making them suitable for dynamic bioreactor environments. Other sensor types may include optical and solid-state sensors, which provide alternatives for specific applications, enhancing process control and optimization in bioprocessing industries.</p></p>
