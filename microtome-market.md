<p><strong>The Microtome Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sliding</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microtome market consists of two primary types: sliding microtomes and rotary microtomes. Sliding microtomes operate with a back-and-forth motion, suitable for cutting thick tissue specimens, commonly used in histology and pathology. In contrast, rotary microtomes employ a circular blade motion, allowing for precise and consistent thin sections, making them ideal for various research and clinical applications. Both types are essential in laboratories for preparing specimens for microscopic examination, each offering unique advantages based on the intended use.</p></p>
