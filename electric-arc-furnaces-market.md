<p><strong>The Electric Arc Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC-EAF</li><li>DC-EAF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Arc Furnaces (EAFs) are primarily categorized into two types: AC-EAF and DC-EAF. AC-EAFs utilize alternating current to melt scrap steel and produce new steel, featuring lower operational costs but typically producing less refined steel. In contrast, DC-EAFs use direct current, offering higher energy efficiency and superior control over the melting process, resulting in higher-quality steel. The choice between AC and DC EAFs depends on specific production requirements, desired steel quality, and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
