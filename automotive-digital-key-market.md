<p><p>The Automotive Digital Key market includes RKES (Remote Keyless Entry System) and PKES (Passive Keyless Entry System). RKES allows users to lock/unlock vehicles remotely using a key fob, often with additional features like starting the engine. PKES enables keyless access and ignition when the key is nearby, using proximity sensors for seamless operation. Both systems enhance convenience and security, with PKES offering a more hands-free experience. The market is driven by advancements in automotive technology and increasing demand for smart vehicle solutions.</p></p>
