<p><p>The Electronic Bionic Eye market encompasses two primary types: external eye and implanted eye. External eye devices are non-invasive, worn outside the body, offering visual assistance through cameras and displays, suitable for individuals with limited vision. In contrast, implanted eye devices are surgically placed within the eye, directly interfacing with the retinal cells to restore vision. Each type serves different needs, catering to varying degrees of visual impairment and enhancing the quality of life for patients with ocular diseases.</p></p>
