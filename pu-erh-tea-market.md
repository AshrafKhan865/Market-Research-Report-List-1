<p><ul><li>Raw Pu-erh Tea</li><li>Cooked Pu-erh Tea</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pu-erh tea market is primarily divided into two main types: Raw Pu-erh and Cooked Pu-erh. Raw Pu-erh, or Sheng, is made from fresh, sun-dried tea leaves and undergoes natural fermentation over time, resulting in complex flavors and aromas as it ages. Cooked Pu-erh, or Shou, involves a controlled fermentation process, making it darker and smoother in taste, appealing for those seeking immediate drinkability. Both types offer distinct experiences, catering to different preferences among tea enthusiasts.</p></p>
