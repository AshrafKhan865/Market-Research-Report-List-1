<p><p>The bakery conditioner market is categorized into two main types: powder and liquid. Powder conditioners offer convenience and longer shelf life, making them ideal for bulk baking and easy incorporation into dry mixes. They enhance dough properties and improve texture. Liquid conditioners, on the other hand, provide direct moisture and uniform distribution, often preferred for artisanal baking and small batches. Both types serve the purpose of improving dough performance, ensuring consistency, and enhancing the overall quality of baked goods.</p></p>
