<p><p>The dental water jet market comprises two main types: countertop oral irrigators and cordless oral irrigators. Countertop oral irrigators are larger, plug-in devices designed for home use, offering multiple pressure settings and water reservoir capacities, ideal for comprehensive oral care. In contrast, cordless oral irrigators are portable, battery-operated devices that provide convenience and ease of use, making them suitable for travel or quick clean-ups. Both types help improve oral hygiene by effectively removing food particles and reducing plaque.</p></p>
