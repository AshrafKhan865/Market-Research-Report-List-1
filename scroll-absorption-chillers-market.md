<p><p>The Scroll Absorption Chillers market includes two primary types: Scroll Chillers and Absorption Chillers. Scroll Chillers use a refrigerant-driven system that employs scroll compressors for efficient cooling with minimal noise and energy consumption. In contrast, Absorption Chillers operate on a thermodynamic cycle utilizing heat sources—like natural gas or waste heat—to drive the cooling process, making them ideal for industrial applications. Both types serve distinct cooling needs, with Scroll Chillers focusing on efficiency and Absorption Chillers emphasizing sustainable energy use.</p></p>
