<p><p>The helical CT scanner market is categorized into double-layer and multi-layer helical CT scanners. Double-layer helical CT scanners utilize two rows of detectors, providing improved speed and resolution for imaging, making them suitable for routine diagnostic applications. In contrast, multi-layer helical CT scanners employ multiple detector layers, enhancing image quality and acquiring data more quickly. This advanced technology is particularly beneficial for complex diagnoses, allowing for detailed assessments in various medical fields, including oncology and trauma care.</p></p>
