<p><p>The Lanthanide Optics Glass market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes in optical applications. Heavy Lanthanum Flint (H-ZLaF) features increased density for specialized applications. Low Softening Point (D-LaK) glass is designed for easier shaping and manufacturing processes, catering to a variety of optical device needs in different industries.</p></p>
