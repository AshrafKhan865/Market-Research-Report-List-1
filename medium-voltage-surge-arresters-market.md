<p><p>The medium voltage surge arresters market encompasses three primary types: embedded, wall-mount, and built-in. Embedded surge arresters are integrated within the equipment, offering compact protection and reducing installation time. Wall-mount types are externally mounted on walls or structures, providing flexibility for retrofitting and maintenance. Built-in surge arresters are designed as part of electrical panels or devices, ensuring streamlined protection with minimal space requirements. Each type addresses specific installation needs and operational environments, enhancing system resilience against voltage surges.</p></p>
