<p><p>The percutaneous nephrolithotomy (PCNL) market comprises several types based on the size of the access sheath used for kidney stone removal. Micro-PCNL utilizes a smaller access sheath for minimal tissue trauma and quicker recovery. Ultra-mini PCNL is designed for slightly larger stones while maintaining a minimally invasive approach, allowing for efficient stone extraction with reduced complications. Mini PCNL offers a balance between traditional PCNL and minimally invasive methods, providing effective treatment for larger stones with less patient discomfort.</p></p>
