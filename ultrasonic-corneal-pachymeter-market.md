<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Corneal Pachymeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Ultrasonic Corneal Pachymeter</li><li>Non-Contact Ultrasonic Corneal Pachymeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Corneal Pachymeter market is divided into two main types: Contact Ultrasonic Corneal Pachymeters and Non-Contact Ultrasonic Corneal Pachymeters. Contact Ultrasonic Pachymeters require direct contact with the cornea to measure its thickness, providing precise and accurate readings but necessitating careful sterilization. In contrast, Non-Contact Ultrasonic Pachymeters use a non-invasive approach, employing ultrasonic waves to measure corneal thickness without touching the eye, enhancing patient comfort and reducing the risk of infection while still delivering reliable results.</p></p>
