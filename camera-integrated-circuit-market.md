Memory Chips store data captured by the camera. Image Processors handle the conversion of images and videos into usable formats, improving quality. IPCSoC (Image Processor System on Chip) integrates multiple functions on a single chip for efficiency. Other market segments include additional components like sensors and interfaces, all contributing to the overall performance and capabilities of camera systems.</p></p>
