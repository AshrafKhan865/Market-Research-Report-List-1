<p><p>The outdoor water purifier market includes various types tailored to different needs. Squeeze-type purifiers work by manual pressure, forcing water through a filter, ideal for lightweight trekking. Pump-type purifiers utilize a hand-operated pump to draw water through a filter, making them suitable for larger groups or longer trips. Suction-type purifiers allow users to drink directly from a water source, using a straw-like mechanism. Other market types encompass innovative designs like UV purifiers or solar-powered models, enhancing portability and convenience.</p></p>
