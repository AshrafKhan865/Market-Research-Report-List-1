<p><p>Tachogenerators are devices used to measure rotational speed and provide feedback for control systems. The market is divided into two main types: DC tachogenerators and AC tachogenerators. DC tachogenerators convert mechanical rotation into a direct current signal, offering precise speed measurement and control, making them suitable for applications requiring high accuracy. AC tachogenerators, on the other hand, generate an alternating current signal, typically used in applications where higher power output is needed and transient responses are critical. Both types cater to various industrial sectors.</p></p>
