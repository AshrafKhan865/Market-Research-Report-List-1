<p><p>The non-visible spectrum image sensor market comprises two main types: linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture light in a one-dimensional array, suitable for applications like barcode scanning and industrial inspections where fast readouts are essential. In contrast, area image sensors capture light in a two-dimensional format, making them ideal for applications such as surveillance and medical imaging, where detailed spatial information and high-resolution images are crucial for analysis and interpretation.</p></p>
