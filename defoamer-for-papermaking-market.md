<p><strong>The Defoamer for Papermaking Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eliminate Bubbles</li><li>Suppress Bubbles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the papermaking market, defoamers are categorized into two main types based on their function. The "Eliminate Bubbles" defoamers actively break down foam layers, preventing bubble formation during the production process. These are crucial for ensuring a smooth flow of pulp and enhancing the quality of the final product. Conversely, "Suppress Bubbles" defoamers help maintain low foam levels without aggressively breaking bubbles. They provide a stable production environment, improving efficiency and output consistency while minimizing foaming issues.</p></p>
