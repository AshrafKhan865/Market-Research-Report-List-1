<p><p>The residential water purifier market features various technologies catering to diverse consumer needs. RO (Reverse Osmosis) water purifiers use a membrane to remove impurities, ensuring high-quality filtration, particularly in areas with hard water. Gravity-based purifiers rely on natural gravitational flow to filter contaminants without electricity. UV (Ultraviolet) water purifiers utilize UV light to neutralize harmful microorganisms, providing safe drinking water. Additionally, multiple technology-based systems combine these methods for enhanced purification, addressing a broader range of water quality concerns.</p></p>
