<p><ul><li>Ordinary X-rays</li><li>Panoramic X-rays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental X-ray machines market can be categorized into two primary types: ordinary X-rays and panoramic X-rays. Ordinary X-rays provide detailed images of specific dental structures, helping in diagnosing issues like cavities or infections. In contrast, panoramic X-rays capture a broad view of the entire jaw, including teeth, bones, and surrounding tissues, allowing for comprehensive assessments in orthodontics and oral surgery. Each type serves distinct diagnostic purposes, enhancing dental care and treatment planning.</p></p>
