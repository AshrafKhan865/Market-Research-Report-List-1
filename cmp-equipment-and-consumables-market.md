<p><strong>The CMP Equipment and Consumables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMP Consumables (Slurry and Pad)</li><li>CMP Equipment (Clustered Tools)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP equipment and consumables market comprises two primary segments: CMP consumables and CMP equipment. CMP consumables include slurries and pads, essential for the chemical-mechanical planarization process, which smoothens and flattens semiconductor surfaces. Slurries contain abrasive particles and chemicals, while pads provide a substrate for material removal. CMP equipment, primarily clustered tools, are automated systems designed to handle multiple wafers simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and throughput in semiconductor manufacturing. Together, they play a crucial role in advanced microfabrication processes.</p></p>
