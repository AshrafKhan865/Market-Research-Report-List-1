<p><ul><li>Mineral Oil</li><li>Synthesis Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine oil market primarily consists of two main types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil, derived from refining crude oil, offers basic lubrication and is often more affordable, but may require more frequent changes. In contrast, synthetic oil is chemically engineered for enhanced performance, providing superior protection, improved fuel efficiency, and longer intervals between changes. The choice between these oils often depends on vehicle requirements, driving conditions, and consumer preferences for cost versus performance.</p></p>
