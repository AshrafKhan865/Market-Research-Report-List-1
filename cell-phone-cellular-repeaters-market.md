<p><strong>The Cell Phone Cellular Repeaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Signal Booster</li><li>Digital Signal Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cell phone cellular repeaters enhance mobile signal strength in areas with poor reception. There are two main types: analog signal boosters and digital signal boosters. Analog signal boosters amplify existing cellular signals, which can lead to broader coverage but may amplify noise and interference. Conversely, digital signal boosters convert signals into digital format before amplification, resulting in cleaner, more reliable connections and minimizing interference. Both types serve to improve communication capabilities in homes, offices, and remote areas.</p></p>
