<p><strong>The Automotive Carbon Brake Rotors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbon-Carbon Matrix</li><li>Carbon-Ceramic Matrix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive carbon brake rotors market is primarily divided into two types: carbon-carbon matrix and carbon-ceramic matrix. Carbon-carbon matrix rotors are made from carbon fibers and a phenolic resin, providing high strength and lightweight properties, making them ideal for high-performance racing applications. Conversely, carbon-ceramic matrix rotors combine carbon fibers with ceramic materials, offering superior thermal stability and resistance to wear, making them suitable for high-end performance vehicles. Both materials enhance braking efficiency and reduce brake fade under extreme conditions.</p></p>
