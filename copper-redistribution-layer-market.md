<p><p>The Copper Redistribution Layer (RDL) market primarily consists of two types: Cu RDL and Cu/Ni/Au RDL. Cu RDL utilizes copper as the main conductive material, offering cost-effectiveness and favorable electrical properties, ideal for high-density interconnections. On the other hand, Cu/Ni/Au RDL incorporates a nickel and gold layer, enhancing reliability, corrosion resistance, and solderability. This makes it suitable for advanced packaging applications in electronics, where enhanced performance and durability are crucial for optimal device functionality.</p></p>
