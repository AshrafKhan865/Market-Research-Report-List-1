<p><p>The rubber-based adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: natural rubber-based adhesives and synthetic rubber-based adhesives. Natural rubber-based adhesives are derived from organic sources and offer excellent elasticity and adhesion properties, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, synthetic rubber-based adhesives are chemically engineered, providing enhanced performance characteristics such as heat resistance, durability, and moisture resistance. Both types serve diverse sectors, including automotive, construction, and packaging, adapting to specific bonding requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
