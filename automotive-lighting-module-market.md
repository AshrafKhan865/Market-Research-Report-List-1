<p><ul><li>Halogen Lighting</li><li>HID Lighting</li><li>LED Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive lighting module market encompasses three main types: halogen, HID (High-Intensity Discharge), and LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting. Halogen lighting is widely used for its affordability and ease of replacement but offers lower efficiency. HID lighting provides brighter illumination with improved energy efficiency, making it preferable for high-performance vehicles. LED lighting is the most advanced, offering superior energy efficiency, longevity, and design flexibility, increasingly becoming the preferred choice for modern vehicles due to its vibrant colors and minimal power consumption.</p></p>
