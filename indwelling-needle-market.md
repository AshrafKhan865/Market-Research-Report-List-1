<p><strong>The Indwelling Needle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Type</li><li>Closed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The indwelling needle market is categorized into open type and closed type systems. Open type indwelling needles allow fluid exchange with the external environment, making them suitable for short-term applications and easy access but posing higher infection risks. In contrast, closed type indwelling needles utilize a sealed system to minimize exposure to contaminants, providing a safer option for long-term use. These distinctions influence market demand, product development, and regulatory considerations in medical settings.</p></p>
