<p><p>The Dual Cure Adhesives market comprises primarily two types: Acrylate Mixture Based and Epoxy Resin Based adhesives. Acrylate mixtures utilize a combination of light and chemical curing processes, providing quick bonding and flexibility in applications such as automotive and electronics. In contrast, epoxy resin-based adhesives offer superior strength and durability, often employed in demanding environments like construction and aerospace. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring efficient bonding solutions through their unique curing mechanisms.</p></p>
