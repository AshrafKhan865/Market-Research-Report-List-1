<p><ul><li>Strand Palletizing System</li><li>Underwater Palletizing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater pelletizer market comprises two primary types: strand pelletizing systems and underwater pelletizing systems. Strand pelletizing systems involve extruding melted polymer through a die, forming strands that are then cut into pellets. In contrast, underwater pelletizing systems directly cool and solidify the polymer pellets underwater, leading to higher quality and more uniform pellet sizes. Both systems cater to different applications in the plastics and polymers industry, emphasizing efficiency and product consistency in the pelletizing process.</p></p>
