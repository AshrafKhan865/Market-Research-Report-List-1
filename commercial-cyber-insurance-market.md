<p><p>The commercial cyber insurance market can be divided into two main types: stand-alone cyber insurance and packaged cyber insurance. </p><p>Stand-alone cyber insurance provides dedicated coverage specifically for cyber risks, addressing data breaches, network interruptions, and liability associated with digital threats. In contrast, packaged cyber insurance combines cyber coverage with other types of business insurance, such as general liability or property insurance, offering a more comprehensive approach. Both types aim to protect businesses from financial losses related to cyber incidents while enhancing their risk management strategies.</p></p>
