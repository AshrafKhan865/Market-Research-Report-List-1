<p><p>The metallic decorating paint market is primarily divided into two types: solvent-based and water-based paints. Solvent-based metallic paints typically offer high durability and a rich finish, making them ideal for exterior applications and high-wear surfaces. However, they can have a strong odor and longer drying times. Water-based metallic paints, on the other hand, are more environmentally friendly, easier to clean, and have quicker drying times, making them suitable for indoor use. Each type serves distinct customer preferences and application needs.</p></p>
