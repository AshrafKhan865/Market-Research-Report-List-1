<p><p>The Agricultural Biological Control Agents market is categorized primarily into three types: Microbial Pesticides, which utilize microorganisms like bacteria and fungi to suppress pests; Biochemical Pesticides, derived from natural materials (e.g., plant extracts, pheromones), that disrupt pest behaviors or physiology; and Plant-Incorporated Protectants (PIPs), where crops are genetically engineered to express pest-resistant traits. Together, these agents offer sustainable pest management solutions that reduce chemical pesticide dependence, enhance crop yields, and promote environmental health.</p></p>
