<p><strong>The Intelligent Bearingless Rotary Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Rotary Encoder</li><li>Incremental Rotary Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intelligent bearingless rotary encoders market is primarily categorized into absolute and incremental rotary encoders. Absolute rotary encoders provide a unique position value for each angle, ensuring precise measurement even after power loss. They are ideal for applications requiring exact positioning. In contrast, incremental rotary encoders generate signals that indicate movement from a reference point, allowing for relative position tracking. They are often used in applications where speed and direction of movement are essential, making both types suitable for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
