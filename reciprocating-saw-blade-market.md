<p><p>The reciprocating saw blade market is categorized primarily into three types: high-speed steel (HSS), cemented carbide, and others. Cemented carbide blades offer superior hardness and wear resistance, suitable for tougher applications. "Other" blades encompass specialty options, including bi-metal and diamond-coated blades, designed for specific cutting tasks across materials like wood, metal, and masonry, catering to diverse industrial and DIY needs.</p></p>
