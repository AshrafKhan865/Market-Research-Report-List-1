<p><ul><li>CMP Slurries</li><li>CMP Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) consumables market consists of two primary types: CMP slurries and CMP pads. CMP slurries are abrasive mixtures used to polish semiconductor wafers and ensure a smooth surface by selectively removing material. CMP pads, made from specialized materials, provide a surface for the slurries to operate effectively and facilitate the mechanical action needed for planarization. Together, these consumables are essential for the semiconductor manufacturing process, enhancing yield and ensuring high-quality finished products.</p></p>
