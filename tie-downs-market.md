<p><p>Tie-downs are essential for securing cargo during transport, with various types serving different needs. Cam straps use a cam buckle for easy tensioning and are ideal for lighter loads. Over-center lever cam style straps provide additional security with a simple lever mechanism, offering more stability for moderate loads. Ratchet straps, featuring a ratcheting mechanism, deliver maximum tightening force and are suited for heavy-duty applications, making them popular in industries requiring robust cargo restraint. Each type caters to specific load requirements and user preferences.</p></p>
