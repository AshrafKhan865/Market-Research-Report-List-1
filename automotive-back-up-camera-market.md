<p><p>The automotive back-up camera market includes three main types: Surface Mounted, Flush Mounted, and License Mounted. Surface Mounted cameras are externally attached, offering easy installation but may be less aesthetically pleasing. Flush Mounted cameras are integrated into the vehicle's design, providing a sleek look and better protection from damage. License Mounted cameras are fixed onto the license plate frame, balancing visibility and convenience. Each type caters to different preferences for installation ease, design, and functionality in enhancing rearview visibility.</p></p>
