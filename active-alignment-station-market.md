<p><p>Active alignment stations are crucial in optical alignment applications, classified by Degrees of Freedom (DOF). The 4 DOF market typically involves positioning components with rotational adjustments in two axes and translation in two axes, catering to simpler systems. The 5 DOF market adds an additional rotational degree, enhancing flexibility for complex setups. The 6 DOF market allows for full control in three-dimensional space, combining translation and rotation in all axes, making it ideal for advanced precision alignment in high-tech industries.</p></p>
