<p><strong>The Spacer Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viscous Spacer</li><li>Turbulent Spacer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spacer Fluid Market includes Viscous Spacer and Turbulent Spacer types, both used in oil and gas well cementing. Viscous Spacer employs high-viscosity fluids to displace drilling mud, ensuring efficient cleaning and separation between cement and mud. Turbulent Spacer uses low-viscosity fluids to create turbulent flow, enhancing mud removal and cement bonding. Both types improve well integrity, with Viscous Spacer ideal for complex wellbores and Turbulent Spacer suited for simpler, high-flow-rate environments, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
