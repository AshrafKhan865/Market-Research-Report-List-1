<p><p>The organic powdered milk market primarily consists of two main types: organic whole powdered milk and organic skim powdered milk. Organic whole powdered milk retains its natural fat content, offering a creamy texture and rich flavor, ideal for consumers seeking a wholesome dairy option. In contrast, organic skim powdered milk has had most of its fat removed, providing a lighter alternative with fewer calories. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers who prefer organic products free from synthetic additives and hormones.</p></p>
