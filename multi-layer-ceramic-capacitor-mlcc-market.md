<p><p>Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCCs) are passive electronic components that store electrical energy. They are composed of alternating layers of ceramic dielectric materials and metal electrodes, which create a capacitor with a high capacitance value in a compact form. MLCCs are widely used in various applications, including consumer electronics, automotive, telecommunications, and industrial equipment, due to their high reliability, excellent frequency characteristics, and ability to withstand temperature variations.</p><p>The MLCC market is experiencing robust growth, driven by the increasing demand for compact and energy-efficient electronic devices.
