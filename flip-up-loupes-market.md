<p><p>The Flip-Up Loupes market primarily consists of two types: Galilean Lens and Prismatic Lens. Galilean Loupes utilize a compact design with a simple lens system, offering a wider field of view and lighter weight, making them suitable for general dental and medical applications. In contrast, Prismatic Loupes incorporate prisms to enhance magnification and facilitate a more ergonomic working posture, though they tend to be bulkier. Each type caters to specific user preferences and operational needs, impacting choice and market dynamics.</p></p>
