<p><p>The smart shoe dryer market is segmented into two main types: embedded and detached. Embedded smart shoe dryers are integrated into footwear, providing built-in drying features for convenience and seamless use. They often incorporate advanced moisture control technology. Detached dryers, on the other hand, are standalone devices designed for drying shoes externally. They offer versatility and can accommodate various shoe sizes, ensuring effective drying without altering the shoes themselves. Both types aim to enhance hygiene and prolong shoe lifespan.</p></p>
