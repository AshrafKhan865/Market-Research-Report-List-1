<p><ul><li>Sterile Type</li><li>Non-sterile Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lab apparel market is divided into two main types: sterile and non-sterile. Sterile lab apparel is designed for environments that require high-level contamination control, such as surgical units or cleanrooms, ensuring the prevention of microbial transmission. Non-sterile lab apparel, on the other hand, is suitable for general laboratory use where contamination risk is lower. This type often includes lab coats and scrubs, providing basic protection without the stringent cleanliness standards associated with sterile apparel. Both types cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
