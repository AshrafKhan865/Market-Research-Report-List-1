<p><strong>The HDPE Rigid Plastic Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Thermoforming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDPE rigid plastic packaging market encompasses three primary manufacturing processes: extrusion, injection molding, and thermoforming. Extrusion involves forcing molten plastic through a shaped die, creating continuous profiles like bottles and containers. Injection molding injects molten HDPE into a mold, producing complex shapes with high precision, ideal for rigid containers. Thermoforming involves heating a plastic sheet until pliable and then forming it over a mold, suitable for producing trays and clamshells. Each method contributes uniquely to the versatility and functionality of HDPE packaging solutions.</p></p>
