<p><strong>The Industrial Mercury Emissions Analyser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Mercury Emissions Analyzer market primarily includes two types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) Mercury Analyzers and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF) Mercury Analyzers. CVAA analyzers detect mercury by measuring the absorption of light as vaporized mercury passes through a light beam, providing high sensitivity and accuracy. Conversely, CAF analyzers utilize fluorescence to detect mercury by emitting light that interacts with mercury atoms, allowing for precise quantification. Both technologies aim to monitor and control mercury emissions in industrial settings, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
