<p><p>Rock Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are specialized equipment used for excavating tunnels through hard rock formations. The market includes two main types: Open-type TBMs, which operate without a protective shield, suitable for stable ground conditions, and Shielded Hard Rock TBMs, designed with a protective shield to support tunnel stability in varied geological conditions. The choice between these types depends on factors like soil characteristics, tunnel diameter, and project requirements, impacting construction efficiency and safety in tunneling projects.</p></p>
