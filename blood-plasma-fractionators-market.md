<p><strong>The Blood Plasma Fractionators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Membrane Plasma Plasma Fractionator</li><li>Non-Membrane Plasma Fractionator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood plasma fractionators market is divided into two main types: membrane and non-membrane plasma fractionators. Membrane plasma fractionators utilize filtration techniques, employing semi-permeable membranes to separate plasma components based on size and molecular weight, ensuring high purity and efficiency. In contrast, non-membrane plasma fractionators use classical techniques like precipitation and centrifugation, focusing on physical and chemical properties to isolate plasma proteins. Both methods aim to optimize the extraction of valuable plasma-derived products for therapeutic and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
