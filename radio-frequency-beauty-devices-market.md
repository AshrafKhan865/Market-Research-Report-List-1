<p><p>The Radio Frequency Beauty Devices market is segmented into three main types: monopolar, bipolar, and multipolar. Monopolar devices use a single electrode for treatment, delivering energy deep into tissues for lifting and tightening effects. Bipolar devices employ two electrodes, focusing energy on the skin's surface for more targeted treatments with less penetration. Multipolar devices combine multiple electrodes, offering a balanced energy delivery that enhances collagen production while providing effective skin tightening benefits. Each type caters to different beauty enhancement needs.</p></p>
