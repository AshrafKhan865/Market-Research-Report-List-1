<p><p>The Feedthrough Ceramic Capacitors market comprises two main types: DC Feedthrough Ceramic Capacitors and AC Feedthrough Ceramic Capacitors. DC Feedthrough capacitors are designed for direct current applications, providing high insulation resistance and minimal leakage, making them suitable for sensitive electronic devices. In contrast, AC Feedthrough capacitors are optimized for alternating current applications, offering reliable performance in filtering and decoupling signals. Both types are crucial in various sectors, including telecommunications, industrial equipment, and high-frequency circuits, enhancing device efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
