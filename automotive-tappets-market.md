<p><strong>The Automotive Tappets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet</li><li>Roller Tappet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive tappets market primarily features two types: flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets, also known as sliding tappets, have a flat surface and rely on friction for motion, commonly found in older engines. Roller tappets, on the other hand, incorporate a roller component that reduces friction and enhances efficiency, often used in high-performance and modern engines. Each type plays a critical role in regulating valve movement and engine performance, influencing the choice based on engine design and application.</p></p>
