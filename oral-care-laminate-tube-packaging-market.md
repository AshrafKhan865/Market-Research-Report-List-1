<p><strong>The Oral Care Laminate Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oral care laminate tube packaging primarily consists of two types: ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminate) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate). ABL tubes feature a layer of aluminum to provide excellent moisture and light protection, making them suitable for products that require enhanced shelf life, such as toothpaste and mouthwash. In contrast, PBL tubes are made entirely from plastic, offering flexibility and lightweight convenience, making them ideal for budget-friendly products. Both types cater to varying consumer needs and preferences in oral care.</p></p>
